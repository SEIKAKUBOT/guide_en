Bot Usage Instructions

1. Overview

Our bot uses support and resistance zones to generate trading signals. It automatically sends signals indicating entry levels, take profit (TP), and stop loss (SL). Remember: the bot is an analysis tool, not a guaranteed profit solution.

2. Risk Management

  • Manage your capital. Never invest more than you are willing to lose. It is recommended to use a small percentage of your capital for each trade.
  • Stop-loss. Always set the stop-loss as indicated by the signal. This helps limit potential losses.
  • Diversification. Do not invest all your capital in one trade or one coin. Spread your risks across multiple positions.
  • Psychology. Do not let emotions influence your decisions. Follow predetermined rules and strategies.

3. Following the Bot's Strategy

  • Strictly follow the signals. The bot provides clear instructions for entry, TP, and SL. It is recommended to strictly adhere to these guidelines.
  • Analyze the chart. Although the bot sends ready-made signals, always review the chart. Evaluate the context and overall market conditions to avoid trades in unfavorable situations.

4. The Bot is Not a Perfect Tool

  • No 100% success guarantee. The bot works on algorithms, and the cryptocurrency market is highly volatile. No tool can guarantee profit on every trade.
  • Markets change. The algorithm may be optimized for specific conditions, so in the event of significant market changes, exercise extra caution.
  • Personal responsibility. You are responsible for your trading decisions. Use the signals as an additional analysis tool, not as an absolute guide to action.

5. Recommendations for Usage

  • Education. We recommend learning the basics of technical analysis to better understand the bot's signals and make informed decisions.
  • Demo account. Before using the bot on a real account, try testing its signals on a demo account.
  • Feedback. If you notice any discrepancies or errors in the bot's performance, contact support. Your feedback will help improve the algorithm.
  • Updates. Stay informed about updates regarding the bot's performance. Over time, the algorithm may be adjusted to enhance its efficiency.

Conclusion

Use the bot as a powerful analysis tool, but always conduct your own market assessment. By following risk management rules and carefully analyzing charts, you can significantly improve your chances of successful trading. Remember: even the best signals require a thoughtful approach and personal responsibility.

This guide will help you effectively use the bot and minimize risks in trading. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to seek assistance.
